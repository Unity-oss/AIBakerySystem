TASTYZ BAKERY — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

== ABOUT TASTYZ BAKERY ==

What is Tastyz Bakery?
Tastyz Bakery is a bakery specializing in freshly baked breads, cakes, pastries, and desserts. We focus on high-quality ingredients and handcrafted baking to deliver delicious treats for everyday enjoyment and special occasions.

What products does Tastyz Bakery offer?
We offer a variety of baked goods including:
- Fresh breads
- Birthday and celebration cakes
- Cupcakes and muffins
- Cookies, Daddies and biscuits
- Doughnuts and pastries
- Custom cakes for events
- Seasonal and specialty desserts

== CUSTOM CAKES ==

Do you make custom cakes?
Yes! We create custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events. Customers can request specific flavors, designs, colors, and messages.

How far in advance should I place a custom order?
We recommend placing custom cake orders at least 24–72 hours in advance depending on the design and size of the cake. More complex designs may require additional time.

== ORDERING ==

Can I order online?
Yes. Customers can place orders through our website or social media pages. Orders can be scheduled for pickup or delivery depending on location.

How do I place an order?
You can place an order by:
- Visiting the bakery in person
- Calling our customer support line
- Sending a message on our social media pages
- Ordering through our website

How do I check the status of my order?
Contact our customer support team with your order number, your name used during ordering, and the date of your order. Our team will provide an update on preparation, pickup, or delivery.

Can I modify my order after placing it?
Yes, you can modify your order before preparation begins. Changes may include flavor adjustments, cake message updates, or pickup time changes. For custom cakes, modifications should be requested as early as possible.

What if I need to cancel my order?
Orders can be canceled if preparation has not yet started. For custom cakes and large orders, cancellations should be made at least 24 hours in advance. Some deposits may be non-refundable depending on the order.

== DELIVERY ==

Do you offer delivery?
Yes, we offer delivery services within selected areas. Delivery fees may vary depending on the distance. Customers must provide accurate delivery address, contact phone number, and preferred delivery time.

What should I do if I missed my delivery or pickup time?
Contact customer support immediately. We will arrange a new pickup time or reschedule delivery if possible. Some delays may affect product freshness.

== PAYMENT ==

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Cash
- Mobile money
- Bank transfers
- Online payments (where available)

Large or custom orders may require advance payment or deposit confirmation before preparation begins.

== PROBLEMS & SUPPORT ==

What should I do if my order is delayed?
If your order is delayed, contact customer support immediately with your order details. Our team will check the status and update you on the estimated completion or delivery time.

What if my order is incorrect?
If you receive an incorrect order, contact customer support as soon as possible and share details of the issue (product type, flavor, or design). We will work quickly to replace or correct the order when possible.

What if my cake or pastries arrive damaged?
If your order arrives damaged, take a photo of the product if possible and contact customer support immediately. Our team will review the issue and provide a replacement or suitable solution.

Do you offer refunds?
Refunds may be issued in situations such as: order cancellation within the allowed time, product unavailability, or confirmed bakery error with the order. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis by our support team.

== DIETARY & STORAGE ==

Do you offer gluten-free or special dietary options?
We offer select dietary options such as gluten-free or reduced-sugar products when available. Customers should request these options when placing an order.

How should I store my cake?
For best freshness: keep cakes in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate cakes with cream or fresh fruit. Consume within 2–3 days for the best taste.

== EVENTS & CATERING ==

Do you cater for events?
Yes! Tastyz Bakery provides bulk orders and dessert catering for events such as weddings, corporate meetings, parties, and celebrations.
